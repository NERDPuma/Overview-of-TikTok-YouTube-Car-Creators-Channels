How to make money on TikTok & YouTube as a car enthusiast

How To Get Started:

Now that you have some ideas of what other successful creators do, you can start brainstorming your own ideas. After that you need equipment, in reality just a phone would do nowadays. Another important thing you need is enthusiasm, you don't want to be boring. Otherwise people would be clicking off of your video pretty quick. Remember, you have to use what you have in the moment, for example if you want to become a reviewer, review your own car or one of your family member's. Dealerships aren't going to let you review one of their latest models if you only have a couple of subscribers or followers, you have to start small and build trust along the way.

How To Grow Your Page/Channel with SEO

Make sure to get to know your future audience, make sure that you use these keywords in your automobile video titles, tags as well as in your video descriptions. Creating video subtitles and closed captions are time consuming however they are very rewarding, as they will help your viewers better in understanding your content and make your automobile videos rank among the top videos as well. Also, make sure to have an interesting thumbnail. Marketing is key to gaining popularity, make sure to use it wisely.

Make Sure To Interact With Your Audience

If you reply to as many comments as you can, your audience will see that you truly care about them and will continue to come back for more content. Also, if you're on YouTube make sure to "heart" comments as much as you can. According to YouTube, “Whenever you heart a viewer’s comment, it makes them 3 times more likely to come back to your channel”. The more connected your audience feels with you, the more likely they'll stick around. Plus, videos with better engagement bring more traffic which means more followers/subscribers, which in turn leads to more money.

Make Sure To Monetize Your Channel (for YouTube only)

Once you have over 4,000 watch hours and 1,000 subscribers you can run ads from Google AdSense on your channel, when the user clicks the ad, you get paid. This is a simple way to earn money before your channel gets even bigger.

Collaborate With Other Creators

Once your channel/page has grown a bit, collaborating with other creators with the same status as you can help boost stats for them and you, it's a win/win. You get more watch time and subscribers and they get the same thing in return. This means you get more traffic, which means more income. This is why collaboration is an amazing thing!
