TikTok Car Community

Skip To YouTube Car Community

The TikTok car community is an interesting community to say the least. You have short videos on car reviews, car mods, car hacks, simulated car crashes, asking car owners what they do for a living, etc. Pretty much anything that is related to cars in anyway is on TikTok. Here are my top 3 favorite car creators on TikTok:

  1. itsdanielmac:

    2. Known as the current automotive king of TikTok. As of December 2021, Daniel Mac has over 12 million followers. He's known as the guy who asks people with nice cars what they do for a living, whether some of his videos are staged or not, they are still very entertaining to watch. He also has a YouTube channel with just over 1 million subscribers, TikTok is more his thing though.

  2. supercarblondie:

    3. Known for being the biggest automotive influencer in the world, and having insane futuristic vehicle appearances on her page, plus having videos of the world's weirdest, rarest, exotic cars as well. Supercar Blondie has over 8 millions followers as of December 2021. Her short videos on these rare cars are entertaining to watch for sure. She also has a YouTube channel with over 7 million subscribers.

  3. tjhunt_:

    4. Personally, I'm a fan of TJ Hunt because his page is informative, has a nice variety of cars, and he can be funny. Plus, he had Sung Kang (Han from Fast & Furious) on his page, and who doesn't like to see the legend himself recreating some moments from the franchise. As of December 2021, TJ Hunt has over 3 million followers, I personally believe he should have more though. He also has a YouTube channel with over 2 million subscribers.

YouTube Car Community

Go Back To TikTok Car Community

The YouTube car community is a bit more "formal" in terms of videos. These videos consist of longer car reviews, car builds, racing videos, DIY fixes. Anything that's car related is on here in more detail. Here are my top 3 YouTube car creators/channels:

  1. Doug DeMuro:

    2. Doug DeMuro is known for reviews on all different types of cars and they're "quirks & features". He reviews cars in a humorous way, sometimes making fun of their design, features, etc. and that's what makes you want to keep watching more. He has over 4 million subscribers and uploads about every 3 days.

  2. MotorTrend Channel:

    3. Being the host of the "World's Greatest Drag Race" almost every year and being the biggest or one of the biggest automotive magazine publishers in the world, it's not a surprise MotorTrend has over 6 million subscribers. Any new car releases you're interested in, MotorTrend will probably have a first look at it. Pretty much every car enthusiast knows about MotorTrend.

  3. Jay Leno's Garage:

    4. With over 3 million subscribers, Jay Leno is well known in the car community. Jay Leno reviews all cars ranging from exotic to classic. He uploads one time every week, and people love it.
